Core issue: You want maximum realistic income with minimum weekly hours, where Perplexity Computer does
most of the cognitive work. Your two strongest assets are (a) lived counselling/coaching credibility under the
Compassionate Rise brand and (b) prompt-engineering / AI-workflow fluency. The strongest play is not in the
counselling lane — that lane is bandwidth-bound by you personally and brand-fragile. The strongest play uses
your AI fluency to sell a productized, AI-heavy service to other small businesses, while a parallel low-effort
digital-product asset slowly compounds inside Compassionate Rise.

My recommendation in one sentence: Launch a productized “AI Front Desk” service for Vernon home-service
businesses (missed-call-text-back, review automation, 24/7 lead qualification) as your single Phase-1 income
engine, and in parallel publish a small psychoeducational regulation guide as a low-touch digital asset for the
Compassion Rise audience. One active experiment, one passive asset. Park everything else.

I am holding to your saved memory rules: narrow over expand, one lane now, stop at clarity, no exhaustive
completion required. The full ranked pool is below for your reference, but I'm not asking you to act on more than
the top 1.

2. Stage 0 — Self-Setup and Limit Check
Mission as I understand it: rank realistic businesses where AI does most of the work, you decide and hold
relationships, Phase 1 target CAD $1–3k/month, Phase 2 CAD $3–8k/month, with ≤10 hrs/week after setup and
≤1–2 parallel experiments.

Tools I have: web search and URL fetch (used; cited below), file reading (used to read your ChatGPT memory
PDF), workspace storage, and reasoning. I cannot log into Facebook Marketplace, Kijiji, your CRM, GBP, or any
account; cannot run paid ads; cannot make calls; cannot guarantee Vernon-specific demand at the granularity of
one zip code.

Files seen: Your ChatGPT memory PDF — the operative directives I am applying are Front-Door Clarity Gate (any
Compassionate Rise public-facing copy must pass orientation/safety/distinctiveness/usability before brand voice
is layered), Precise High-Judgment Writing (fact > inference > conclusion > next step), Overextension Interruption
(narrow, don’t widen; one lane; stop at clarity), and Communication Growth Audit tone (calm, candid, no fluff, no
fake neutrality). I am also assuming Kevin Smith / Vernon BC / Compassion Rise per the prompt defaults.

Discipline I will hold: facts get citations; inferences and assumptions are labeled; revenue numbers above public
benchmarks are flagged as estimates; self-audit at end.

3. Stage 1 — Definition of “AI-Runnable Business” and
Scoring Model
Working definition (recommendation, not fact): An AI-runnable business is one where Perplexity Computer (and
adjacent LLM/no-code tools) can perform ≥70% of recurring research, drafting, qualification, documentation, and
customer-message work; human time is concentrated in approvals, relationship moments, pricing/contract
decisions, and brand-sensitive content; and the business can be tested with under CAD $500 and under 10 hours
of human work in two weeks.

Scoring Rubric (designed framework — assumption)
I weight non-equally because, given your stated constraints, hours/week and skill fit dominate. Total = 100.

Research plan (executed)
I scanned five domains: (a) AI-agent businesses for SMBs, (b) Vernon/Okanagan local business landscape and
STR market, (c) digital-product marketplaces in mental-health/coaching adjacent niches, (d) Etsy/local
cedar-planter pricing, (e) productized service pricing (local SEO, GBP, AI-SDR, review management, proposal
writing, couples coaching). Signals tracked: stated price ranges, real case studies with revenue, complaints in
forums, evidence of demand.

12 Opportunity Zones
Zone A — AI-receptionist / missed-call-text-back / review automation for home-service trades
Facts: AI appointment-setter market pricing is $200–$500/month per business (AiMarketer Pro). Documented
case study: AI Growth OS for a plumbing company booked 12 appointments and closed $8,310 in 30 days (Jason
Fleagle). Skilled-trades shortage in Canada is well-documented and pushes incumbents toward outsourcing
front-desk and lead capture (CBC, Remodelers Advantage). Vernon Chamber of Commerce directory shows 8+
contractor categories and 9 home-and-garden firms locally (Vernon Chamber).
Inference: Local trades miss after-hours calls and lose leads; a $300–$700 CAD/mo retainer is well within their
tolerance because one captured job often pays for a year of service.
Assumption: ~30 viable target businesses within 60 km of Vernon.
Initial note: Strongest single zone for Kevin given AI fluency, sales background, and local relationships.

Zone B — Google Business Profile / local SEO / review-request retainers
Facts: Canadian local-SEO retainers commonly $499–$999/month, with the small-business sweet spot at
$500–$1,500/mo (NoxRank, Optuno, Storyteller Media). GBP review/management tools start around
$5–$75/seat/mo wholesale (The CMO).
Inference: Local businesses underuse GBP — explicit complaint pattern in r/GoogleMyBusiness (Reddit).
Assumption: Vernon trades are below-average on GBP optimization (consistent with smaller-city pattern; not
measured).
Initial note: High overlap with Zone A; usually bundled.

Zone C — Vernon Airbnb / STR co-host & data services
Facts: Vernon STR market: 216 active listings, $19,193 avg annual revenue, 50.1% occupancy, $165 ADR; supply
grew 47.9% YoY (AirROI). BC STR regulations are strict and shifting; principal-residence rules in nearby Kelowna
change June 1, 2026 (Destination BC, City of Vernon).
Inference: Compliance + listing optimization + AI-driven dynamic pricing is a real local pain.
Assumption: Hosts will pay $200–$400/listing/mo for managed listing/compliance/messaging — typical industry
pattern, not Vernon-measured.
Initial note: Real but more operationally hands-on than Zone A; regulatory risk material.

Zone D — Psychoeducational digital products (workbooks, regulation guides)
Facts: Etsy “emotional regulation workbooks” sell at $1–$20, with bundles up to ~$20.44 and 1k+ review counts on
top items (Etsy, Etsy listing). Digital-mental-health market is large and growing 16–20% CAGR (Towards
Healthcare, Research and Markets).
Inference: Low-priced single PDFs do not produce meaningful income; bundled or premium ($30–$129) products
with email funnel are needed.
Assumption: Kevin can credibly produce a high-quality product in this niche given counselling background.
Initial note: High brand fit, low active hours; not a Phase-1 income engine alone but excellent compounding asset.
